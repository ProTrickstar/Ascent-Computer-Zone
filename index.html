OUR STORY

From A Vision To A Trusted Technology Partner.

Ascent Computer Zone began with a simple mission — to empower people through computer education. Founded by Mr. Sanjay Kumar, the journey started in Nawada, Bihar, where students were trained in computer fundamentals, Tally, AutoCAD, typing, and professional IT skills.

In 2005, Ascent expanded to Baddi, Himachal Pradesh, evolving into a complete technology solutions company. Today, we provide enterprise IT infrastructure, computer sales and service, surveillance systems, networking, security solutions, and custom computing for businesses and individuals.
