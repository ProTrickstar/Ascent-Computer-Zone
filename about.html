WHERE IT ALL BEGAN

Every Great Journey Begins With A Vision.

Long before Ascent Computer Zone became a trusted technology solutions provider, it began with a simple yet powerful mission— helping people build a better future through computer education.

Founded by Mr. Sanjay Kumar in Nawada, Bihar, Ascent started as a computer training institute where students learned practical skills including Tally, AutoCAD, professional typing, Microsoft Office and advanced computer applications.

What started as a classroom soon became something much bigger.

2005

A NEW CHAPTER

Expanding Beyond Education.

In 2005, Ascent Computer Zone expanded to Baddi, Himachal Pradesh.

The company evolved from computer education into a complete technology solutions provider, serving industries, pharmaceutical companies, businesses and individuals.

Today our expertise spans enterprise IT infrastructure, networking, surveillance systems, custom-built computers, annual maintenance, repairs and complete technology support.

THE FOUNDER

Meet The Man Behind Ascent Computer Zone.

Behind every successful organization is a vision. Behind Ascent Computer Zone is Mr. Sanjay Kumar, whose journey began with a belief that technology should educate, empower and create opportunities.

Beginning as a computer educator in Nawada, Bihar, he dedicated himself to helping students build practical digital skills before expanding the vision into a complete technology solutions company.

Today, that same commitment continues through enterprise IT solutions, computer systems, networking, surveillance, infrastructure and customer-first service.
